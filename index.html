My passion for Tech.

Since childhood, I've been captivated by computers and gadgets, delving into their intricacies. This early fascination ignited my passion for technology, shaping my journey into the digital realm.

Little did I know, my childhood obsession with technology would pave the way for my venture into design. Experimenting with gadgets honed my eye for detail, fueling my journey into technology and design.


Receiving my first computer in grade 8 marked the start of my digital journey. I explored various tools and immersed myself in HTML, Excel, and Ms-Word.

During my Advance Computer studies, I seized the chance to enroll in a graphic design course. There, I was introduced to Adobe Photoshop and Pagemaker, gaining essential skills for my design journey.

Amidst the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020, I began exploring web development and digital marketing tools, driven by my fascination with human behavior and decision-making in the digital sphere.

This curiosity led me to ponder key questions: How do people make online decisions? What impact do digital marketing techniques have on consumer choices? How do color choices influence user preferences?

In 2021, I committed to becoming a versatile developer, delving into Bootstrap, JavaScript, and various web development tools.

Applying my skills to personal and freelance projects, I served as both a designer and no-code developer. Acquiring fundamental CSS and HTML knowledge, I streamlined collaboration with developers and sought mentorship for growth.

Expanding my expertise, I'm pursuing advanced training for certification while interning in Flutter application development. This dual focus empowers me to thrive in frontend and backend realms, advancing my career in digital innovation.
